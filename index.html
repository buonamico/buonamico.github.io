About Me

25+ years in IT, from software engineering to leading organizational evolution.
15+ years driving transformation across startups, enterprises, and consulting environments.

I lead the evolution of how organizations build and deliver products in increasingly complex, distributed, and fast-changing environments.

My work operates at the intersection of Agile leadership, systems thinking, and emerging AI-enabled ways of working — where the challenge is no longer adopting frameworks, but designing systems that can continuously adapt, scale, and improve.

I started my career as a Systems Engineer, building software systems from the ground up, and evolved into leading large-scale transformation across startups, enterprises, and consulting environments in Latin America and globally.

I help shape the evolution of hybrid product and project execution models, partnering with leaders and teams to improve how work flows across complex Product, Engineering, IT, and PMO ecosystems.

My focus is on enabling organizations to evolve from process-driven execution toward adaptive, outcome-oriented, and scalable systems that thrive amid uncertainty, rapid technological change, and the need for faster, better-informed decision-making.
